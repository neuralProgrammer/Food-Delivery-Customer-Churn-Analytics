# Food Delivery Customer Churn Prediction & Business Analytics Platform

## Overview

An end-to-end Product Analytics project inspired by real-world food delivery platforms such as Eternal (formerly Zomato), Swiggy, Uber Eats, and DoorDash.

The objective is to build a production-style analytics platform that enables:

- Customer churn prediction
- Customer behavior analysis
- Business intelligence dashboards
- Product analytics
- Feature engineering
- Experimentation (A/B Testing)
- Executive decision support

Rather than focusing solely on machine learning, this project emphasizes business impact, product thinking, SQL, and data-driven decision making.

---

## Dataset

This project uses the **Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Dataset**, adapted to simulate a food delivery marketplace.

Conceptual mapping:

| Olist | Food Delivery |
|--------|---------------|
| Seller | Restaurant |
| Product | Food Item |
| Order | Food Order |
| Customer | Customer |
| Review | Rating |
